⭐ All Features at a Glance

🕐
Clock & Date
DS3231 RTC module keeps time accurately without internet. Syncs with NTP when WiFi is available. Displays 24-hr or 12-hr format.
🌤️
Live Weather
OpenWeatherMap API fetches real Quezon City weather every 10 minutes. Shows temperature, humidity, wind, and a weather icon.
📅
Schedule & Reminders
JSON-based daily planner. Color-coded events: green (done), blue (upcoming), amber (urgent). Reminder alerts 10 minutes before.
💬
Motivational Quotes
Database of 100+ curated quotes. Rotates daily at midnight. Displayed subtly near the top-center of the mirror.
📰
PH News Ticker
Scrolling bottom ticker fetches top 5 Philippine headlines via RSS. Refreshes every 30 minutes. Source: Rappler, GMA News.
🌡️
Room Sensor
DHT11 on Arduino reads room temperature (°C) and humidity (%) in real-time. Data sent over serial to Raspberry Pi every 5 seconds.
👁️
PIR Wake / Sleep
HC-SR501 PIR motion sensor detects presence. Display wakes in under 1 second. Auto-sleeps after 60 seconds of no motion. LED turns green when active.
👤
User Personalization
Configurable user profile (name, schedule, preferences). Greeting adapts to time of day. Future: face recognition via Pi camera.

🖼️ Mirror Layout Explained

↖️ Top-left
Clock & Date
Large, glanceable time. Date below in small text. Powered by DS3231 RTC for offline accuracy.
↗️ Top-right
Weather
Temperature + condition icon. Right-aligned so it doesn't crowd the clock. Refreshes every 10 min.
↙️ Bottom-left
Schedule
Today's top 3 events with color-coded dots and times. Never clutters the center reflection area.
↘️ Bottom-right
Room Sensor
Live °C and % humidity from DHT11. Right-aligned. Updates every 5 seconds from Arduino serial.
⬆️ Top-center
Quote (faint)
Very low opacity motivational quote. Subtle — you see it when you look for it, invisible otherwise.
⬇️ Bottom-center
Greeting
"Good morning, Juan!" — personalized and time-aware. Small italic text, unobtrusive.
↔️ Bottom edge — News ticker
PH News RSS Feed
Thin scrolling bar across the very bottom. Ultra-dim text. Fetches top PH headlines via RSS every 30 minutes. You can glance at news without any extra device.
